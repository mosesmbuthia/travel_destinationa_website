My 5 dream vacation locations

I would visit Diani to enjoy the white-sand beach and the crystal-clear turqoise waters and the lush greenery. Experience the swahili culture and explore the coral reefs.

  • Visit and Explore Simba Hills National Reserve
  • Sunbathing on the Beach

I would visit Dubai to experience extra-ordinary feats of architecture and engineering, indulge with the beauty of arabian desert and experince world class shopping and dining.

Activities

I would visit the Great Wall of China to get a chance to walk in the footsteps of history and witness the sheer scale and engineering prowess on ancient wonder as well as experience the breathtaking views surrounding the landscapes.
